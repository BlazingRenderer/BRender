Fogdem Demo

This technical demonstrator indicates the ability of BRender to produce
depth of field effects.

CONTROLS

To Control Fogdem

Under DOS

'H':    Toggle Help text on/off
'F':    Toggle Fogging on/off
'M':    Toggle scene light on/off
'S':    Toggle spot light on/off
'G':    Add/Remove ground model
'P':    Fade palette


Basic Mouse Controls

Left Button: moves object in XY plane
Right Button: Rolling ball motion
Both Buttons: zoom in/out in Z plane
