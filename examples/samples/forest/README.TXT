Forest Demo

This demo shows BRender's abilities to z-sort a scene of a space ship
manoeuvring through a forest.

CONTROLS

Basic Mouse Controls:

Left button: Move system in the XY plane.
Right button: Rolling ball motion.
Left & Right buttons: Zoom in/out in the Z plane. 

To Control The Demo:

'A':            Freezes a frame of the animation
SPACEBAR:       Switches between camera modes
'Q':            Quit Demo
'C':            Toggle auto camera mode switching
'1'/'0':        Move forwards/backwards
'4'/'6':        Move left/right
'8'/'2':        Move up/down
